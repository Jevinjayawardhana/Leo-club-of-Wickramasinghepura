FIT CORE

“FIT CORE” was successfully conducted by the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura of Leo District 306 D6 as a meaningful wellness initiative aimed at promoting physical and mental well-being among youth. The project primarily focused on a guided yoga session, incorporating breathing techniques, flexibility-enhancing postures, and relaxation practices designed to improve balance, mindfulness, and overall body strength.

Held on 01st November, 2025 at Huskies Court, Kotte, the session brought together enthusiastic participants who actively engaged in beginner-friendly to intermediate yoga flows. Attendees received valuable guidance on stress reduction, proper posture, and integrating simple yoga routines into their everyday lifestyle.

The program not only encouraged physical well-being but also fostered mental clarity, inner calmness, discipline, and self-awareness among participants. By completing “FIT CORE”, the club successfully contributed to nurturing a healthier, more mindful community and inspiring young individuals to embrace yoga as a positive habit in their daily lives.

On 01 st November 2025
Huskies Court - Kotte

WAVE WHISPERS 3.0

“WAVE WHISPERS 3.0” was conducted as an environmental conservation initiative by the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura of Leo District 306 D6. The project was organized with the objective of enhancing coastal cleanliness, supporting marine ecosystems, and encouraging community-driven environmental stewardship.

The program took place on 16th November, 2025 at Wellawatte Beach, Colombo, where volunteers and community participants united for a comprehensive beach clean-up campaign. Activities included the systematic collection, segregation, and responsible disposal of waste materials found along the shoreline. This collaborative effort contributed to minimizing marine pollution while improving both the aesthetic appeal and environmental health of the coastline.

Beyond the physical clean-up, “WAVE WHISPERS 3.0” served as a platform to promote awareness on sustainable waste management and the impact of plastic pollution. The initiative successfully cultivated leadership, teamwork, and a sense of environmental accountability among all participants. Through this project, the club reaffirmed its commitment to safeguarding natural resources and inspiring long-term Eco-conscious behaviour within the community.

On 16 th November 2025
Wellawatte Beach - Colombo

WAVE WHISPERS 3.1: MEALS FOR PAWS

“WAVE WHISPERS 3.1: MEALS FOR PAWS” was carried out as a compassionate community initiative by the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura of Leo District 306 D6. This project was organized in response to the recent floods, with a primary goal of supporting street animals affected by the disaster and ensuring their access to proper nourishment.

Conducted on Sunday, 30th November 2025, the project centered around the Athurugiriya area, where volunteers came together to collect ingredients, prepare nutritious meals, and distribute them to animals in need. Key food items included rice, pumpkin, dhal, and canned fish—ensuring a wholesome and filling meal for distressed street animals during severe weather.

The initiative delivered crucial immediate relief while also encouraging a deeper sense of empathy, humane responsibility, and community engagement among participants. It highlighted the importance of extending kindness to all living beings, especially during challenging times. Through the successful completion of “WAVE WHISPERS 3.1: MEALS FOR PAWS,” the club demonstrated its commitment to animal welfare and strengthened the community’s resolve to care for vulnerable animals during crises.

On 30 th November 2025 (Sunday)
Athurugiriya, Malabe and Kaduwela Areas

Waves of Support 1.1

“Waves of Support 1.1” was successfully organized and conducted by the Leo Club of Wickramasinghapura of Leo District 306 D6 as an urgent humanitarian initiative to assist communities severely affected by the destructive weather conditions that swept across Sri Lanka in late November 2025.

With countless families facing hardship due to heavy floods and adverse weather, the club promptly mobilized resources to provide immediate relief. On 28th November 2025, a collection of essential items—including dry rations, biscuits, milk packets, sanitary items, and other emergency supplies—was donated to the Colombo Red Cross Branch. These supplies were carefully selected to meet the urgent needs of individuals and families struggling during the crisis.

Members of the Leo Club of Wickramasinghapura played an active role in gathering contributions, sorting items, and ensuring the timely handover of the relief packs. Their dedication, teamwork, and compassion ensured that support reached the affected communities swiftly.

This project not only demonstrated the club’s commitment to serving the nation in times of distress but also highlighted the importance of collective action, empathy, and youth leadership during national emergencies. Waves of Support 1.1 stands as a meaningful effort to bring hope, comfort, and essential aid to those who needed it the most during a challenging period for the country.

On 28 th November 2025
Colombo Red Cross Branch
