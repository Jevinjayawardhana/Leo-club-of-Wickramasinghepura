Youth Fusion


Project Youth Fusion was successfully concluded on the 3rd of April 2025 at Sri Subuthi Maha Vidyalaya, Battaramulla. This impactful initiative was a collaborative effort between the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura and our parent Lions Club, the Lions Club of Wickramasinghepura.
The project aimed to empower A/L students by conducting a comprehensive career guidance programme, helping them explore various academic and professional pathways. In addition to the informative sessions, we also prioritized student health by facilitating medical checkups through a mobile dental and eye  checkup bus.

On 01 th April
Sri Subuthi Maha Vidyalaya - Battaramulla

Fitcore - Phase 01


On the 6th of April 2025, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura successfully concluded Fit Core Phase 1 at the scenic Wallawatte Beach. This invigorating project was organized with the aim of promoting physical wellness and fostering camaraderie among Leos. The morning was filled with energy and enthusiasm as participants engaged in a dynamic exercise session led by experienced fitness enthusiasts. Members from our club, along with fellow Leos, actively took part in the session, embracing the opportunity to focus on health and mindfulness amidst nature.

On 06 thApril
Wallawatte Beach

Gilanopasthana: A Heartfelt Awurudu Celebration with Elders On the 25th of April 2025, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura successfully concluded Project ගිලානෝපස්ථාන at the Sheeloo Elders Home in Hokandara.
In celebration of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year, we organized a warm and traditional අවුරුදු කෑම මේසය for the beloved elders residing there. The table was filled with delicious festive treats, spreading joy and nostalgia among the participants. As a gesture of love and respect, we also donated new clothes to the elders, bringing smiles and heartfelt gratitude.

On 25 th April
Elders Home - Hokandara

Fit Core Phase 2


On the 2nd of May 2025, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura successfully completed Fit Core Phase 2 at Kelaniya Maha Vidyalaya, Kelaniya. As part of our ongoing effort to promote physical wellness and youth development, this phase focused on enhancing the school’s sports facilities. We proudly donated a range of essential sports equipment, including footballs and other training gear, to support the students in their athletic pursuits. The initiative aimed to encourage active lifestyles among schoolchildren and provide them with the tools needed to engage in sports and physical education more effectively.

On 02 nd May
Kelaniya Maha Vidyalaya - Kelaniya
