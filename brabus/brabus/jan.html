Diriya Diwimaga 2.3


On the 15th of January, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura, in collaboration with the Saheli Foundation, successfully carried out Diriya Diwimaga 2.3 at Castle Street Hospital, Colombo. This initiative aimed to support expectant mothers by providing them with essential care packages and equipping them with vital knowledge on postpartum well-being.
Understanding the challenges that come with motherhood, the project included an awareness session on postpartum depression and other crucial aspects of postnatal care. This session not only helped educate mothers on mental and physical health after childbirth but also emphasized the importance of emotional support during this transformative period.
The distribution of care packages was a heartwarming gesture aimed at easing the journey of these soon-to-be mothers. These packages contained essential items to support both the mother and baby, ensuring they receive the necessary care during and after pregnancy. Diriya Diwimaga 2.3 was a testament to the power of compassion and collaboration, highlighting the impact of collective efforts in creating a positive change in the community. The Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura remains committed to uplifting lives, and this initiative was another step toward ensuring a healthier and more informed future for mothers and their newborns.

On 15 th January
Castle Street Hospital - Colombo

On January 19th, 2025, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura, in collaboration with the Rotaract Club of the University of Moratuwa and Sumbal Surf Café, successfully conducted Punchi Rasata Rasa Padamata at LOLC Child Care Center, Madiwela. This heartwarming project aimed to bring smiles to the faces of children through an interactive entertainment session while also ensuring they were well-nourished throughout the day. The event was filled with engaging activities that encouraged creativity, laughter, and joy among the children. Through games, storytelling, and interactive performances, we created an environment where they could express themselves freely and enjoy a day of happiness. Additionally, we provided them with nutritious lunch and snacks, ensuring their well-being and energy levels were maintained throughout the program. This project was a true testament to the power of collaboration, as it brought together passionate individuals dedicated to making a difference in the lives of young children. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our fellow Leos and the outsiders who were presented to the project for their unwavering support and commitment to this meaningful initiative.

On 19 th January
LOLC Child Care Center - Madiwela

Smart Cents


Financial literacy is a crucial skill that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about managing their finances effectively. Recognizing its importance, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura, in collaboration with AIESEC in Colombo Central and the Leo Club of Colombo Host, successfully conducted Smart Cents, an online project series aimed at enhancing financial literacy skills. Through this initiative, participants gained valuable insights into essential financial concepts, including budgeting, saving, investing, and responsible spending. The sessions were designed to provide practical knowledge that could be applied in everyday life, helping individuals navigate their financial journeys with confidence.
The interactive nature of the series encouraged meaningful discussions, allowing participants to engage with experts and peers to deepen their understanding of financial management. By addressing common financial challenges and misconceptions, Smart Cents played a vital role in promoting financial responsibility among young individuals. As a club dedicated to empowering communities, the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura is proud to have contributed to building a financially literate society through this initiative. The success of Smart Cents highlights the impact of collaboration and knowledge-sharing in shaping a more financially secure future for all.

Wave Whispers 2.3


Wave Whispers 2.3, organized by the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura, aimed at raising awareness about mangrove conservation. This phase focused on a social media awareness campaign, highlighting key aspects such as the importance of mangroves, their various types, ecological benefits, and the urgent need for protection. Through engaging and informative posts, the project educated the public on how mangroves contribute to coastal protection, biodiversity, and climate resilience. By leveraging digital platforms, Wave Whispers 2.3 reached a wide audience, inspiring individuals to take action toward preserving these vital ecosystems.
