ABOUT US

Founded in 1992/93 as an Omega Leo Club under Lions Club of Wickramasinghepura, Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura has been led by capable youth leaders, notably Leo Ayanthi Wickramasinghe. It consistently raised societal standards and club excellence, making a positive impact until 2022/23.

OUR PREVIOUS WORKS

The Legacy of the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura Continues. The Dedication and the Commitment of the Wickipura Leos have showcased the Potential and Skill they possess from the Activities and the Projects done in the Leoistic Year 2023/24. It was a successful year for the Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura since the Club was recognized as the 2nd Runners-Up in the Leo Multiple 306. The Projects had a better impact on the Community and Society as well, and Wickipura Leos will always be there to make a sustainable and more meaningful impact on the Community and the Country.
