Our History


Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura is an Omega Leo Club which is a great initiative of Lions Club of Wickramasinghepura established in the 1992/93 during the District Presidency of Prabha Dharmarathne. Leo Club of Wickramasinghepura belongs to Leo District 306 C1 which comes under Leo Multiple District 306.
The young leader with a great caliber, Leo Ayanthi Wickramasinghe took the challenge in leading the club at the inception. Following her foot steps and with the guidance of the leaders in the parent Lions club, the talented youngsters led the club since 1993/94 to 2020/2021 who were capable of lifting up the standards of many groups in the society while improving the standards of the club as well the members.

About Leoism


Leo Club is a youth organisation of Lions Club International. The acronym L.E.O stands for Leadership, Experience, Opportunity. The Leo Club objective is, to provide the youth of the world with an opportunity for development and contribution, individually and collectively, as responsible members of the local, national and international community.
Leo Club encourages the youth to develop leadership qualities by participating in social service activities. They are dependent on a Lions club to sponsor and initiate a Leo club. Leo Club members are addressed as “Leos.” They conduct various projects in the fields of health care, elders, children, differently abled, literacy and education, and self-development. LEO Club is the largest youth club of voluntary character in the world, present in 140 countries with over 160,000 members.
